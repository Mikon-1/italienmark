BEST BREAD IVE EVE RHAD IN MY LIFE!!! again made by my mom :)) could also be used for dinner Fast bread (One large "wheel")
  • 50 g. butter
  • 2 dl. milk
  • 1 1/2 dl. natural yogurt
  • 25 g. yeast
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 1 tsp. coarse salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp. dried rosemary
  • 350 g. wheat flour (approx. 5 3/4 dl.)
Brushing: Milk Garnish: Dried rosemary Baking time: Place the bread in a cold oven for approx. 20 min. at a setting of 225 degrees. Melt the butter in a saucepan and add the milk. Pour the mixture into a bowl and add the yogurt. Stir in the yeast. Add the remaining ingredients (keep a little flour). Knead the dough well. Take the dough out onto a floured surface and knead it well. Add the rest of the flour if needed. Shape the dough into a round, flat loaf (approx. 28 cm in diameter) and place it on a baking sheet lined with baking paper. Score the bread almost all the way through to form eight triangles. Brush the bread with milk and sprinkle with rosemary. Bake in the middle of the oven.

Cranberry smoothie (4 people) 350 g cranberries, thawed if frozen 425 ml cranberry juice, chilled 300 ml. natural yogurt 2-3 tbsp. clear honey Place the berries and juice in a blender and blend until smooth. Add the yogurt and honey and blend again until combined. Taste and add more honey if needed. Pour into chilled glasses and serve.
